My top 3 best movies of all time

1. 3 Idiots

The film explores themes of friendship, education system pressures, and the pursuit of one's dreams. It received critical acclaim for its humor, performances, and social message, becoming one of the highest-grossing Indian films at the time of its release in 2009.

2. Dangal

The film highlights the struggles and triumphs of the Phogat family as the daughters strive to excel in the sport, ultimately bringing pride and recognition to their father and the nation. "Dangal" was widely acclaimed for its powerful performances, inspiring storyline, and strong portrayal of female empowerment.

3. Bajrangi Bhaijan

The film explores themes of humanity, compassion, and the emotional bond that develops between Pavan and Munni as they face various challenges during their journey. It also touches upon the issues of cross-border relations and cultural differences.
